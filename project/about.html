About the Mother's Legacy Team

We are a group of Saladmaster health advocates based in Abu Dhabi dedicated to empowering families with simple, practical cooking techniques that preserve nutrients and reduce reliance on unhealthy oils. Our approach centers on family, respect for traditional flavors, and modern healthy cooking methods.

Our mission

To inspire people to eat better, live better and achieve the life they desire. Change your Cookware, Change your Life!
